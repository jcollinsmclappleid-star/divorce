GENERAL COPY RULES — apply throughout

Replace all instances of:
"model" or "modelling" → "see" or "understand"
"calculate" or "calculator" → "picture" or "analysis" where it fits naturally
"tool" → "platform" or remove entirely
"scenario" used alone → "settlement option" where it refers to outcomes
"financial settlement calculator" in body copy → "financial clarity platform"

The tone throughout should be:
- Direct and calm, not clinical
- Empathetic but not soft — these people need confidence, not sympathy
- Specific rather than general — name the real fears (the house, the pension, running out of money)
- Never salesy — state true things plainly and let them land

Do not change: pricing amounts, feature functionality, legal disclaimers, methodology page, or any calculator input/output labels.
