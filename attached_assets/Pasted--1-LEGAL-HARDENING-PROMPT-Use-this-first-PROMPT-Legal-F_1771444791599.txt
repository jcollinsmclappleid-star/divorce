	•	Advising on settlement fairness

	•	Illustrative financial modelling
	•	Educational scenario testing
	•	User-driven input based
	•	Not advice
	•	Not a recommendation
	•	Not predictive of legal outcome

	4.	Ensure:
	•	Disclaimer appears in header (short form)

	•	Terms acceptance before payment
	•	Clear statement that users should seek independent legal/financial advice
	•	Mortgage affordability framed as “indicative market benchmarks” only
	5.	Provide:
	•	A rewritten master disclaimer
	•	Suggested Terms of Use clauses (limitation of liability, no reliance, no advisory relationship)
	•	Wording changes in tracked format (Before → After)

Maintain premium tone. Avoid over-alarming language.

Deliver structured output:
	•	Executive Summary
	•	Risk Areas Identified
	•	Recommended Copy Changes
