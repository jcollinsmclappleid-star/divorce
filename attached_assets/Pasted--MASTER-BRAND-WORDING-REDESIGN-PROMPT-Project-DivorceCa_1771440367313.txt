3️⃣ LANGUAGE CONTROL RULES (APPLY SITE-WIDE)

Replace any advisory phrasing with modelling phrasing.

Use:

“Model”
“Illustrate”
“Quantify”
“Compare”
“Assess indicative sustainability”
“Evaluate structural implications”

Avoid:

“Should”
“Best”
“Ensure”
“Protect”
“Guarantee”
“Know you’ll be okay”
“Qualify”
“Approved”

Risk phrasing must say:
