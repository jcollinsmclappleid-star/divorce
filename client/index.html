UK Divorce Financial Settlement Calculator

Divorce Calculator UK is a free financial settlement calculator for people going through divorce or separation in England and Wales. Enter your assets, property, pensions, savings and debts to model how a court would likely divide your finances under the Matrimonial Causes Act 1973.

What does this calculator cover?

  • Property and house division including mortgage buyout calculations
  • Pension sharing and pension offsetting
  • Savings, investments and joint accounts
  • Debts, loans and credit cards
  • Spousal maintenance and clean break orders
  • Income assessment using 2025/26 HMRC tax and National Insurance rates
  • Financial Sustainability Indicator showing 5-year post-divorce projections

How does it work?

  1. Enter your financial details — assets, debts, property, pensions and income
  2. The calculator applies English and Welsh family law principles to model likely outcomes
  3. View your free basic results instantly, or upgrade for full scenario comparison and PDF report

Is it free?

Yes. The basic calculation is completely free with no account required. A full analysis including scenario comparison, stress testing, 5-year financial projections and a downloadable PDF Structured Financial Brief is available for £79 with 12 months unlimited access.

Who is this for?

Anyone going through divorce or separation in England and Wales who wants to understand their financial position before consulting a solicitor, negotiate a fair settlement, or reduce legal costs by arriving at consultations already informed.

Is this legal advice?

No. This tool provides financial modelling based on general principles of English and Welsh family law. It is not legal advice. For advice specific to your situation, consult a qualified family law solicitor.
